Our mission statement

As part of a global chamber network, an important business community in Romania and competent advisors, we promote bilateral economic relations and help our members, customers and partners to develop new potential for their international business.

We are the central engine for the development of German-Romanian economic relations and connect the business worlds of both countries . As a service provider , we provide professional support for companies from both countries in order to successfully and sustainably establish and expand business in the respective market.
